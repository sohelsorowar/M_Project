Staff Meeting and Notice

Here admin can call meeting automatically and decision can be save into databas.

In this web app admin can send mail to the registerd members automatically or manually.

After meeting decesion will be recorded to database so that admin can pull any data if necessary

Instructions

1. To add members click on admin and than click on Committee_members and than click on ADD COMMITTEE_MEMBEER+ on the top-right.

2. To create meeting click on Create_Meeting.

3. To see the list of meetings clik on Meeting_List.

4. To update go to Meeting_List and click on Meeting_SubHeading(blue colored) and then update.

5. To delete any meeting go to Admin than click on Create_Meeting and select the meeting you want to delete and press delete button on bottom-left.
